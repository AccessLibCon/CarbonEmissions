Carbon Emissions and Access Conferences

We're thinking critically about carbon emissions of national conferences.

In 2019, Peter Binkley warned "We have to stop meeting like this" citing evidence of greenhouse gas emissions resulting from business-related air travel.

He could not have foreseen a pandemic would move the conference online. We now have the opportunity to explore the impact of that change.
